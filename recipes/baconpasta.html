Baconpasta

Description

A spicy and warm meal with protein for the entire family

Ingrendients

  • 2 packets of bacon
  • 250 gram of Penne Pasta
  • 1 Large onion
  • 2 Large Red Chilipeppars
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon into smaller pieces
  2. Cut the onion in halves
  3. Cut the Red Chilipeppars in halves
  4. Add the onion and red chilipeppars to a food processor and process until smooth
  5. Start Boiling the Penne Pasta until desired texture
  6. In a frying pan, add butter and fry the bacon until they have nice color
  7. Add the mix of onion and red chilipeppars
  8. Add the crushed tomatoes and let it all stir for a few minutes while your penne pasta is getting ready
  9. Enjoy your spicy weekday treat!
