Fried Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt, or to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 3/4 inch thick pork chops

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet
  2. Combine flour, season salt, salt and pepper in a paper or plastic bag
  3. Place pork chops in the bag and shake to coat. Shake off excess flour
  4. Fry chops in hot oil until golden on the outside and juices run clear, 4 to 5 minutes per side
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
